i What is Series & Movie Tracker?

Your personal entertainment library — organized exactly the way you want it.

The Series & Movie Tracker helps you organize, track and manage every series and movie you watch in one place. Whether you're binge-watching the latest release or catching up on classics, you stay in full control of your viewing experience.

With multiple profiles, detailed statistics, progress tracking, ratings and more, it transforms how you manage your collection — and everything is stored locally in your browser, so your data stays completely private.

1 Getting Started

Create your profile

Open the menu (☰) in the top-right, expand Profile and click Change User. Create a profile with a name and a picture.

Add your first title

Expand the Content section and click + Add Content. Choose Series or Movie, then fill in:

  • Title and cover image URL
  • Genre and content type
  • Status — Watching, Want to Watch, or Completed
  • Progress (seasons, episodes, time)
  • Streaming service (for series)

Organize your collection

Use the filter buttons at the top to switch between Currently Watching, Want to Watch and Completed. The search bar and Filters & Sort menu help you find anything fast.

💡

The top bar stays integrated at the very top of the page and turns into a floating bar the moment you scroll. While at the top, use Hide search & filters to collapse it for a cleaner view.

2 Notifications & Calendar

Never miss a new release. Open the Notification Calendar from Settings → Notification Calendar in the menu.

  • Click + Add Notification to schedule a reminder for a new season or episode of any title in your list.
  • Every entry can be changed later — press Edit next to it to update the date, type, season or episode.
  • Entries dated before today are hidden automatically, so the list only ever shows what's still upcoming.
  • When a reminder's date arrives, the matching title moves to Want to Watch and is updated to the newest season/episode for you.

3 Customization

Make the tracker feel like yours from the side menu:

  • Themes — switch between Dark, Light, or a fully Custom theme with your own background and accent colors.
  • View Size — Small, Normal, Large, or a custom size from the Filters & Sort menu.
  • Collapsible Sections — every section in the menu has a tappable header button; tap it to expand or collapse its options.
  • Reload — click the logo at the top any time to reload the page; your saved data stays intact.

4 Backup & Data

Export regularly

Everything is stored locally in your browser, so clearing cookies or site data will erase your tracker. Use Export List in the menu to create backups you can keep safe.

Auto-Export

Enable Auto Export in Settings and pick an interval, so your data is backed up automatically while you use the site.

Import anywhere

Use Import List to restore a backup or move your collection to another device or browser.

⚠️

Auto-export only runs while the site is open. For long-term safety, export a manual backup now and then and store the file somewhere reliable.
