Version 1.15 July 19 2026

Adding Content

  • Quick-add button: A floating red + button on the main page opens the add dialog directly — no more digging through the menu.
  • Smart auto-fill: Type two letters of a title and the app guesses what you mean (e.g. “squi” → Squid Game) and fills in the cover, genre, content type, seasons, episodes, runtime, rating, age, description and streaming services automatically.
  • TVMaze fallback: Titles that aren't in the local catalogue are looked up live on TVMaze, so brand-new and obscure series auto-fill too — including their season and episode counts.
  • Real genres: The catalogue was enriched with genuine genres from Wikidata (movies) and TVMaze (series), so auto-filled genres are finally correct — no more “Star Wars” as a genre.
  • Locked content type: When adding a Movie only “Movie” is selectable (and the same for Series), so the type can no longer be picked wrong by accident.

Tracking & Watchlist

  • Hover action bar: Hovering a card pops up an action bar (Episode Watched / Edit / More / Delete) that stays visible until you click it or hover another card. The separate “+1” circle button was removed in its favour.
  • Styled season dialog: Finishing a season now shows a proper in-app dialog (“Next season” / “Extend season”) instead of a browser popup; the watch-timer prompts got the same treatment.
  • Easier watchlist reordering: The broken up/down arrows are gone — drag rows anywhere with the mouse or with the ☰ handle on touch, with live preview, edge auto-scroll and a clear lifted style while dragging.
  • “What tonight?” picker: A button on the Watchlist that picks something random from your list, optionally filtered by genre and how much time you have.
  • Year in Review: A “Wrapped”-style yearly recap under the Watch Timeline: episodes and movies watched, watch time, busiest months, top genres, most-watched titles and your longest daily streak.

New Pages & App

  • Landing page: The old Application Summary became a proper landing page (landing.html) that explains the app. First-time visitors are redirected there automatically; the “Open the app” button remembers you've been there. Its menu links were removed everywhere.
  • Interactive onboarding: A first-run guided tour points out profiles, adding content, the Marketplace, search and statistics — skippable, and it only ever shows once.
  • Installable app (PWA): With a web manifest and offline caching, the tracker can be installed on your home screen or desktop and keeps working offline.
  • Rewritten Guide: The Guide was rebuilt from scratch — every feature explained with what-it-does summaries and step-by-step instructions.

Marketplace

  • Filters that stick: Category, sort, provider, status, age and view choices are saved and restored on your next visit.
  • Bigger detail popup: The title popup grew, shows more data (genres, popularity, original title) and has a Watch trailer button linking to YouTube.
  • Nicer “Add title” popup: The add-to chooser was redesigned with clear colour-coded options and descriptions.
  • Matching look: The Marketplace now uses the same background and surface colours as the tracker, theme switching is instant (the laggy blur effect was dropped), and the S&M logo sits in the red box next to “Tracker”.

Design & Theming

  • Visual refresh: Cards and sections got depth (gradients, borders, shadows, hover lift), status-coloured card edges, chip-style stats, pill-shaped search/filters/badges, a smaller logo and tighter header.
  • Steady top bar: The sticky search bar keeps exactly the same size while scrolling instead of shrinking.
  • Colored Filters toggle: An optional setting that tints the status filter buttons in their theme colours.
  • Better color picker: Custom-theme colours now offer preset swatches and a hex field alongside the colour wheel.
  • Whole-card zoom: The custom view size now scales the entire card, so smaller sizes genuinely fit more titles on screen.
  • Popup redesign: All dialogs (cookie notice, import/export, add dialogs) follow the new rounded style with pill buttons.

Fixes

  • Streaming icons repaired: Broken logo URLs were replaced with a reliable icon service, so service icons load again in the edit menu and on cards; the Disney+ and HBO Max watch links were fixed too.
  • Gradient text icons: Icons no longer disappear when gradient text is enabled under the Light or Dark theme.
  • Timeline background: The Watch Timeline panel now matches the other panels instead of using a darker odd-one-out background.
  • Sidebar polish: The gradient-text toggle now looks like the other menu items, the intro video is off by default, and the Auto-Export interval only appears while Auto-Export is switched on.
  • Credits: Footer updated to “made with love by twillightmay & x4ndrrr”.

Marketplace

Tracker

  • Collapsible top bar: The collapsed search pill can now be tapped to close and tapped again to reopen.
  • View sizes: Small / Large now also rescale the content box (not just the image) so more items fit on screen; Normal is unchanged.
  • Full time spent: Cards now show total time for the whole title rather than per episode.
  • Age information: Age ratings now appear in the detail view and are stored with each title.
  • Gradient text option: An optional, customisable gradient-text mode for headings and key labels across the public pages (off by default).

Marketplace Generator (private maintainer tool)

  • Strict genre fetch: Choose a single genre and the generator only pulls titles of that genre.
  • Collection modes: Add existing titles into a collection, or create a franchise and pull all matching titles into it.
  • Rate-limit modes: Switch between Normal / Bulk / Emergency from the dashboard; the hard ceiling stays at 40 requests/second (never above 50).
  • Auto-monitoring: Titles are refreshed automatically once they pass 30 days, with manual refresh still available.
  • Age & release detection: The generator now records age certifications and a “not released yet” status.
  • Dashboard polish: Light gradients and small animations across the dashboard.
Version 1.12 June 17 2026

Content, Tracking & Sync

  • Runtime / Time to Finish: New field on every title — average runtime per episode for series, total runtime for movies — shown directly on the cards.

  • Top layer for popups: Opening any popup, modal or the side menu now hides and disables the search/filter bar so the opened layer is always on top and nothing behind it is clickable.
  • Unified buttons: Bulk-action and small buttons now match the main button style, and the Guide and Changelog pages use the same button design with a single “Back to Tracker” link.
  • Mobile polish: Two-column layouts for bulk actions, detail actions and stats, with 44px touch targets throughout.
  • Marketplace fixes: Fixed a stray notification flashing on load, added an overwrite confirmation when re-adding an existing title, capped provider selection (max 5), and the search bar/toolbar hide while a detail dialog is open.

Pages

  • Legal: Removed the Contact & takedown section.
  • Application Summary: Removed the Marketplace Generator section (it is a private maintainer tool).
  • Start page & Export: Reworked the start-page call-to-action and empty-state copy, and removed recommended-folder-structure references from Export.

Marketplace Generator (private maintainer tool)

  • Higher throughput: Maximum request rate raised to 40 requests/second, with the daily limit set to unlimited (recommended ~1,000/day for politeness).
  • Add-content helper: The dashboard now has an Add Content section with ready-to-copy command lines, so entries can be created without writing commands by hand.
  • Redesigned dashboard: The dashboard UI was reorganised and cleaned up — same features, better grouped — with monitoring, coverage, rate-safety and one-click scan controls.
  • Collections & categories: Added a collection builder and de-duplicated category generation so franchises group cleanly without redundant tags.

Version 1.0 Initial Release

Hello Fellas! Today I want to show you my newest project - Serien.html. Here you can create your own list of series you are watching, have watched, or want to watch.

Core Features

  • Series Tracker Only: Track TV series with series-specific fields like seasons, episodes, and progress tracking
  • Multiple Status Categories:
    • Currently Watching (red badge)
    • Want to Watch (blue badge)
    • Completed (green badge)
  • Comprehensive Series Information: Title, cover image, genre, language, IMDB rating, season/episode progress, total seasons/episodes, and time spent watching
  • Interactive UI: Expandable cards with "More" button for extra details, edit and delete options per series
  • Three View Sizes: Small, Normal, and Large display options
  • Priority Watchlist (Drag & Drop): Add series to a queue, reorder by dragging, separate "Watchlist" section

Search & Filtering

  • Live search across all titles and genres
  • Filter by status (All, Watching, Want, Completed)
  • Sort options: Default order, Title (A-Z / Z-A), Most/Least time spent

Data Management

  • Add new series via modal form
  • Edit existing series
  • Delete series with confirmation
  • All data persists in browser Cookies
  • No login required - works offline

Progress Tracking

  • Visual progress indicators (season/episode)
  • Time spent displayed in hours/minutes
  • Progress badges and status labels
  • Timeline display (S1 E5 format)

Use Cases

  • Track what TV series you're currently watching
  • Maintain a watchlist of series you want to watch
  • Record completed series
  • Organize your viewing queue with drag & drop
  • Quick access to series details and progress

Interface Design

  • Single dark theme only (no theme switching in v1.0)
  • Clean, focused design for series tracking
  • Responsive layout that works on mobile and desktop
  • No streaming service icons or selection (added in v1.1)

Technical Features

  • Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript implementation
  • No external dependencies
  • Browser-based storage
  • Drag & drop functionality
  • Form validation
  • Responsive design
