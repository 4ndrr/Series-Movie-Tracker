01 What this app is

Series & Movie Tracker is a personal, browser-based tool for keeping track of the films and series you watch. It runs entirely in your browser — there is no account, no server that stores your library, and no analytics tracking you across the web.

The Marketplace is an optional area where you can browse pre-configured titles and add them to your personal library with one click. The catalogue shown there is built from metadata provided by The Movie Database (TMDB).

Formally: this software is provided as a personal, non-commercial hobby project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any of the data providers, studios, or streaming services named within it.

02 TMDB data & attribution required

The movie and series metadata in the Marketplace — titles, descriptions, release years, ratings, episode counts and cover images — is supplied by The Movie Database (TMDB) through their public API.

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

We follow TMDB's API Terms of Use. In particular: TMDB is credited as the source wherever its data appears, the official TMDB logo is shown unmodified, metadata obtained from the API is not retained longer than permitted (it is periodically refreshed), and the data is never used to train machine learning or AI systems.

TMDB's terms are available at themoviedb.org/api-terms-of-use and their logo & attribution guidelines at themoviedb.org/about/logos-attribution.

03 OMDb (optional ratings) optional

The content-generation tool used by the maintainer can optionally pull additional ratings (such as IMDb scores) from the OMDb API. This is turned off by default and, when used, is limited to rating values only.

OMDb's poster service is not used. OMDb data, where present, is credited to OMDb and remains subject to OMDb's own terms at omdbapi.com.

04 Images & posters

Cover images and backdrops shown in the Marketplace are loaded directly from TMDB's image servers. They are linked, not copied — this app does not host, re-upload, or redistribute poster files.

TMDB does not own the underlying poster artwork; rights in that artwork belong to the respective studios, distributors, and rights-holders. The images are displayed here for identification and reference within a personal tool.

05 Your data & privacy

Everything you create in this app — your library, ratings, notes, watch history, profiles and settings — is stored locally in your own browser using localStorage. It stays on your device.

  • No personal account is required and none is created.
  • Your library is not uploaded to any server controlled by this project.
  • No advertising or cross-site tracking is performed.
  • You can export your data at any time from the Import / Export menu, and you should do so to keep a backup.

Because data lives in your browser, clearing your browser storage, using private/incognito mode, or switching devices will mean your library is not there. Export regularly to avoid losing it.

Note that when cover images and metadata load from TMDB (and optionally OMDb), your browser contacts those third parties directly, and their own privacy practices apply to those requests.

06 Cookies & local storage

This app does not use tracking cookies. It uses browser localStorage purely to remember your library and preferences (such as your chosen theme) between visits. This information is technical and necessary for the app to function and is never sent to an advertising network.
