Track everything you watch

Series & Movie Tracker is a free, private tracker for your series and films. Log your progress episode by episode, plan what to watch next, and see your viewing stats — all stored on your own device, no account needed.

Free · No sign-up · Works offline

What it does

Progress tracking

Keep your exact season and episode per series. One click on “Episode Watched” logs it and moves you to the next one — season rollovers included.

Smart adding

Type two letters and the tracker guesses the title, then fills in the cover, genre, seasons, rating and streaming service for you. Or browse the built-in Marketplace catalogue.

Watchlist & queue

Plan what's next with a drag-to-reorder watchlist, and let “What tonight?” pick something random that fits your mood and the time you have.

Stats & Year in Review

Watch time, genre breakdown, an activity timeline and a yearly “Wrapped”-style recap of everything you watched.

Profiles

Separate profiles for everyone in your household — each with their own list, watchlist and statistics.

Your look

Dark, light or fully custom themes with your own colors — plus notifications for new episodes and an installable app experience.

How it works

1

Create your profile

Open the app and set a name and picture. No e-mail, no password — it's all yours.

2

Add what you watch

Use the + button and let autofill do the work, or add popular titles from the Marketplace in one click.

3

Track & enjoy

Log episodes as you watch, keep your watchlist in order, and watch your stats grow.

Your data stays yours. Everything is stored locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Use the export feature to make backups or move your list to another device.
