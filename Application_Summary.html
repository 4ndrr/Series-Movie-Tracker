What it is

The project has two parts. The Tracker is the main app you use day to day. The Marketplace is an optional in-app catalogue of ready-made titles you can add to your library in one click.

Tracker features

Track everything

Add movies, series and anime. Organise them by status: Watching, Want to Watch, and Completed.

Ratings & notes

Give each title your own rating, write personal notes, tag it, and record the actors you care about.

Progress & stats

Track seasons and episodes watched, total watch time, completion rate, and your watch history.

Notifications calendar

Set reminders for upcoming releases. Entries are editable, and past dates are hidden automatically.

Queue

Build a watch queue and reorder it with drag & drop so you always know what's next.

Multiple profiles

Keep separate libraries for different people, each with its own data and stats.

Light & dark themes

Switch between a Netflix-style dark theme and a clean light theme; your choice is remembered.

Import / Export

Export your whole library to a file for backup, and import it again on any device.

Search & filters

Search across your whole collection and filter or sort by type, status, rating and more.

Marketplace

A built-in, browsable catalogue of pre-configured titles. Add any of them to your library in one click — no manual typing of seasons, covers or ratings.

One-click add

Each title comes pre-filled with metadata. Adding it never overwrites ratings or notes you've already made.

Curated collections

Whole franchises in one place — Star Wars, the MCU, DC, Christopher Nolan and more — addable as a set.

Rich detail view

Posters, descriptions, ratings, runtime, episode counts and where to watch — all in one tidy panel.

Search, filter, categories

Browse by category (Movies, Series, Anime, Trending and more) or search the whole catalogue instantly.

Privacy & data Local-first

Stays on your device

Your entire library lives in your browser's local storage. Nothing is uploaded to a server run by this project.

No tracking, no ads

No advertising and no cross-site tracking. Local storage is used only to remember your library and settings.

You own your backup

Export any time to keep a safe copy. Clearing browser data removes your library, so back it up regularly.
